Main Characters

Yuji Itadori

Character Traits

  • Extremely high physical abilities and cursed energy
  • Possesses immense strength, speed, and stamina
  • Can host Sukuna without losing control
  • Has a strong sense of justice and empathy
  • Quick learner in jujutsu techniques

Satoru Gojo

Character Traits

  • Widely recognized as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer
  • Possesses the Six Eyes and Limitless cursed technique
  • Can use Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void
  • Confident and sometimes arrogant personality
  • Cares deeply for his students and their development

About Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Sh≈çnen Jump since March 2018.

The story follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to eliminate a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host.

Jujutsu Kaisen is a story about powerful curses and the jujutsu sorcerers who exorcise them. It features dynamic battles, unique cursed techniques, and a fascinating power system.
